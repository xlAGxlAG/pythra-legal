Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026
Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Welcome to PolymAI (“the Extension”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use our Chrome browser extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

  • Collect or store your Polymarket account passwords
  • Access or store wallet private keys, seed phrases, or credentials
  • Track your browsing activity outside of Polymarket
  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties

2. Information We May Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide the core functionality of the Extension.

2.1 Local Storage Data

Stored locally in your browser to improve user experience:

  • User preferences (e.g., market interests)
  • UI settings (sidebar visibility, layout options)
  • AI chat history (if the feature is enabled)

Note: This data is stored locally on your device and is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

2.2 Account and Identity Information (Optional)

If you choose to sign in using Google OAuth, we may access:

  • Email address
  • Display name

This information is used only for authentication and preference synchronization. We do not access your Gmail content, contacts, or other Google account data.

2.3 Page Context Data (Polymarket Only)

To provide contextual AI insights, the Extension may read information from Polymarket pages only, including:

  • Market titles, outcomes, and prices
  • Structured page content required for analysis

This data is processed in real time and is not stored long-term.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

We use collected information solely to:

  • Display AI-powered insights within Polymarket pages
  • Save user preferences and settings
  • Provide AI chat and market analysis features
  • Improve product performance and usability

4. Data Storage and Security

  • By default, data is stored locally in the browser
  • Any data transmitted to backend services is sent over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

We may use trusted third-party services in limited cases, such as:

  • Authentication providers (e.g., Google OAuth)
  • AI service providers (processing text input only, without sensitive personal data)

All third-party services are required to comply with applicable privacy and security standards.

6. Chrome Extension Permissions

The Extension requests only the permissions necessary to function properly, including:

  • activeTab: Access the current Polymarket page when the user explicitly interacts with the Extension
  • storage: Save user preferences locally
  • scripting: Inject UI and analysis components into Polymarket pages
  • notifications (optional): Send notifications only with explicit user consent

Permissions are never used without user interaction or consent.

7. User Control and Choices

You may at any time:

  • Disable or remove the Extension via Chrome settings
  • Clear locally stored data through your browser
  • Opt out of optional features such as notifications or sign-in

When the Extension is removed, locally stored data is deleted.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Extension or official channels.
