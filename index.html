Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 26, 2026
Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Welcome to Pythra Insights (“the Extension”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use our Chrome browser extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

  • Collect or store your Polymarket account passwords
  • Access or store wallet private keys, seed phrases, or credentials
  • Track your browsing activity outside of Polymarket
  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties

2. Information We May Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide the core functionality of the Extension.

2.1 Local Storage Data

Stored locally in your browser to improve user experience:

  • User preferences (e.g., market interests)
  • UI settings (sidebar visibility, layout options)
  • AI chat history (if the feature is enabled)

This data is stored locally on your device and is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

11. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Pythra Insights Chrome Extension. It does not govern the data practices of third-party websites, including Polymarket itself.
